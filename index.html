A 3-Day Convention!

This summer, beginning on Friday, July 14, 2023 through Sunday, July 16, 2023, at the The Liacouras Center in Philadelphia, PA. I attended a 3 day convention entitled, "Exercise Patience!"
Ironically, my name means Patience Matters, it was a much needed and timely convention. Patience is a quality that I am continuously working on and will continue to work on :-).

Each day had a practical theme.

    Daily Themes:

  • Friday: Learn how exercising patience can help you reach your goals.
  • Saturday: How can showing patience help you improve your relationships with family and friends?
  • Sunday: If you pray to God for help, what can you expect? Find out in the Bible-based talk “Will God Act in Your Behalf?”

    • Each day the subject of "patience" was discussed by a collection of public symposiums.

      Daily Symposiums:

  • Friday:
      There Is an Appointed Time for Everything
      Imitate Those Who Inherit the Promises Through Patience
      What Creation Can Teach Us About Jehovah's Timing
  • Saturday:
      We Recommend Ourselves as God’s Ministers . . . by Patience
      With Patience, Put Up With One Another in Love
      Better to Be Patient Than to Be Haughty in Spirit
  • Sunday:
      A Pattern of Exercising Patience​—The Prophets
      With Patience, Put Up With One Another in Love
      Better to Be Patient Than to Be Haughty in Spirit

    • Each day the information was provided by a collection of audio and video presentations.
